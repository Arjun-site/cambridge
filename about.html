ABOUT CAMBRIDGE

About Cambridge

Founded in 2015, Cambridge International School, Palampur, embodies the vision of Shri Sanjiv Sharma, Chairman of Katiani Educational Trust, to deliver world-class education in a nurturing and inspiring environment..

With state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to foster openness and creativity, the school serves as an integrated learning space. Our extensive play areas feature facilities for basketball, tennis, badminton, skating, volleyball, and athletics, ensuring every child experiences a well-rounded development. For our youngest learners, the “Kiddy Program” introduces early exposure to sports, promoting physical activity and teamwork from the start.

At CISP, students are at the heart of everything we do. Our learning framework is built around personalised education, strengths, all while being supported in achieving their goals.

Our spacious, modern classrooms and open environs provide the perfect setting for intellectual challenges confidently and become well-rounded individuals.

CISP is affiliated with and recognised by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi, offering classes up to Grade XII in three streams—Science, Humanities, and Commerce. As one of the premier CBSE schools in the region, we follow the Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE) system, setting a benchmark for quality education and innovation in teaching practices.
